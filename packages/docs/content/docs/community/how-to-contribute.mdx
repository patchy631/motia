# Contributing to Motia

Thank you for your interest in contributing! Motia thrives because of the developer community—your efforts directly help make Motia a better, more powerful framework. Here’s how you can contribute:

---

## Reporting Issues

Found a bug, have a feature request, or spotted something unclear? Report it:

- Visit our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/MotiaDev/motia/issues).
- Search existing issues to avoid duplicates.
- Clearly describe your issue or request, including:
  - Steps to reproduce the bug.
  - Your environment (OS, Motia version, Node.js version).
  - Screenshots or error messages if applicable.

---

## Submitting Pull Requests

We warmly welcome pull requests for bug fixes, improvements, documentation, and new features:

1. Fork the [Motia repository](https://github.com/MotiaDev/motia).
2. Clone your fork and create a feature branch from `main`. Use descriptive branch names like `fix-typo-in-docs` or `add-new-feature`.
3. Make your changes following our coding standards and best practices.
4. Write tests for your changes, if applicable.
5. Commit your changes and push them to your forked repository.
6. Push your branch to your fork and open a pull request targeting the `main` branch of the Motia repository.

Provide a clear description in your pull request, explaining what you've done and why it matters.

---

## Improving Documentation

Good documentation is critical to a great developer experience. As such, it is a great way to contribute to Motia.

- If you notice errors, typos, or areas that need clarification, submit a pull request with improvements.

- Documentation source files are located in the `packages/docs/content` directory.

- For significant changes, consider opening an issue first to discuss your ideas.

---

## Sharing Examples & Use Cases
If you have built something interesting with Motia or have a real-world use case to share, we would love to showcase it in our [Examples](https://github.com/MotiaDev/motia/blob/main/docs/examples) section. You can contribute your examples by submitting a pull request to the [Motia Examples repository](https://github.com/MotiaDev/motia-examples).

---

## Creating Integrations & Plugins
Expand Motia’s ecosystem by creating integrations with popular tools and platforms:

- Examples include databases, APIs, cloud services, and more.

- Reach out via Discord for assistance or simply open a PR.

- Your contributions help others learn and inspire new ideas.


---

## Community & Advocacy
Help grow the Motia community:

- Star ⭐️ and watch our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/MotiaDev/motia).

- Follow us on [X](https://x.com/motiadev) to stay in the know.

- Engage, help others, and share your experiences on our [Discord](https://discord.gg/CGRU6Xzm).

- Write tutorials, blog posts, or record videos about your Motia projects. We’d love to share your work with our community!

- Help new contributors by reviewing PRs and providing feedback.

- Answer questions and guide others in the [Discord](https://discord.gg/CGRU6Xzm) community.

We deeply appreciate every contribution. Let’s make the future happen today!
